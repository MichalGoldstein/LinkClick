Our History

You may already know us from our work in text recognition and file processing. We started with a simple goal: to make extracting text and URLs from PDFs and images effortless. We saw the need for a tool that was fast, accurate, and easy to use, and we built our platform to fill that gap.

Since our launch, we have helped thousands of users convert their files into usable text formats, saving time and effort.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make text extraction as simple and efficient as possible. Whether you're extracting text from a scanned document, a screenshot, or a PDF, we provide an intuitive tool that gives you clean, accurate results with minimal effort. Plus, we offer an option to download extracted text as a .txt file for easy use.

About Us
