About LinkClick

Welcome to LinkClick! I created this tool to make extracting text from images and PDFs quick and hassle-free. Whether you need to copy a URL from a screenshot, grab text from a scanned document, or extract content from a file, LinkClick makes the process seamless

One of the main goals of LinkClick is to improve accessibility. Many files and images contain important information that isn’t easily readable by screen readers or assistive technology. By converting images and PDFs into text, LinkClick helps make data more accessible to the vision-impaired community, as well as anyone who benefits from readable text instead of images.

I built this platform because I wanted a simple, no-frills way to turn images and PDFs into usable text. No sign-ups, no complicated steps—just upload your file and get your text.

If you need support or have any questions, feel free to contact us at linkclinksite@gmail.com.
